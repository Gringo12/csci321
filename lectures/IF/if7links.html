• How to play interactive fiction:
    • http://www.eblong.com/zarf/if.html#dreamhold The Dreamhold: This is the BEST way to learn about IF, a game designed to show you how to play IF, written by one of the greatest IF authors of all time!
    • https://emshort.blog/ Emily Short's website, containing much good advice and some great games. Her games usually focus on people and interactions rather than physical puzzles. If you think you'd like to get into that, check her out.
